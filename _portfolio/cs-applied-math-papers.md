## Overview

A curated collection of cutting-edge computer science and applied mathematics papers focusing on machine learning and artificial intelligence. Each topic includes foundational knowledge, frontier research papers, and future research directions.

## Curation Philosophy

This collection is organized around three principles:

1. **Building Foundational Knowledge**: Resources to understand the mathematical and conceptual foundations
2. **Understanding Frontier Research**: Latest papers pushing the boundaries of each field
3. **Identifying Limitations & Directions**: Critical analysis of current challenges and future opportunities

## Research Topics

### Neural Tangent Kernels (NTKs)

Understanding infinite-width neural networks through kernel methods.

**Foundation**: Functional analysis, Gaussian processes  
**Key Papers**: NeurIPS 2018 foundational work on convergence and generalization  
**Topics**: Lazy training, infinite networks, kernel methods

### O(3)-Equivariant Deep Networks

Rotation and reflection invariant networks for 3D data.

**Foundation**: Group theory, 3D rotation groups, orthogonal groups  
**Applications**: Molecular property prediction, physical system simulation  
**Recent Work**: Tensor-network formalism, sparse kernel generators

### Real-time Adaptation Laws for Neural Networks

Dynamical systems approach to neural network optimization.

**Foundation**: Differential equations, Lyapunov stability, nonlinear dynamics  
**Key Concepts**: Lyapunov functions, control theory  
**Applications**: Real-time neural network adjustment

### Model Compression via Numerical Linear Algebra

Efficient neural network compression using matrix approximation techniques.

**Foundation**: Numerical linear algebra, matrix decomposition  
**Reference**: Trefethen and Bau's numerical analysis textbook  
**Applications**: Model deployment, edge computing

### Graph Neural Networks (GNNs)

Neural networks for graph-structured data.

**Foundation**: Graph theory, message passing, node embeddings  
**Applications**: Recommender systems, computer vision, molecular modeling  
**Key Concepts**: Permutation equivariance, graph pooling

### Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)

Incorporating physical laws into neural network training.

**Foundation**: Partial differential equations, automatic differentiation  
**Applications**: Scientific computing, inverse problems, topology optimization  
**Challenges**: High-frequency problems, noisy data, computational cost  
**Future**: PINN 2.0 with neuro-symbolic integration, quantum acceleration

### AI for Health (AI4Health)

Machine learning applications in healthcare.

**Foundation**: Medical image analysis, diagnostic reasoning  
**Applications**: Personalized medicine, drug discovery, treatment planning  
**Challenges**: Data dependency, bias, clinical integration  
**Direction**: Explainable AI, human-in-the-loop systems
